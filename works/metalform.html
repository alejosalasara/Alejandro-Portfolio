02 Overview

what is metalform

Metalform SAS is a Manizales metalworking shop: precision fabrication, industrial maintenance and mechanical services for plants and operations across Caldas. A capability-led B2B supplier whose pitch is precision, uptime and on-time delivery.

03 Objective

the objective

a site a procurement buyer trusts in ten seconds, capability up front, and "request a quote" impossible to miss. no brochure-ware; a working sales tool.

01

prove the capability

put the shop, the machines and finished work in front of the buyer before a single word of marketing.

02

make the services legible

collapse a vague list of "what we do" (fabrication, maintenance, machining) into a structure a buyer can scan and match to their job.

03

drive the quote

one obvious path to a request-for-quote, with the spec fields that make the first reply useful.
