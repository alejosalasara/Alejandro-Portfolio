what is imfit

IM FIT is a Colombian direct-to-consumer wellness brand selling natural, INVIMA-certified supplements: depuratives, fat-burners, calmants and nutritional support, alongside hot & cold therapy gels. It sells nationwide with medical advisory built into the buying journey.
