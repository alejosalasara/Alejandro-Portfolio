what is la fábrica

La Fábrica Vape Store is a multi-brand vape and smoke shop based in Manizales, Caldas. Devices, pod systems, e-liquids, disposables, accessories and smoke-shop goods, sold online across Colombia and through physical points in Manizales and Villamaría.
